One of my biggest passions is running.

I have been consistently running for about 11 years (since I was in Middle School). I joined my school's Cross Country team and also competed in the Track & Field Program. This fascination with running continued throughout High School and college. I am still a very active runner, completing 3-7 miles everyday. Running helps me clear my mind and think. I am able to view dilemmas from different angles and broaden my outlook. Running also allows me to experience nature. I enjoy veering off the beaten path and exploing new nature trails. Programming is a very sedentary job and I feel as though there needs to be time to "unplug" from the digital world. When I'm running, I'm free: disconnected, deep in thought, and open to exploration.
