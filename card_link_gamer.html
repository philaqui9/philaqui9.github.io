A Gamer at heart.

I love videogames. I love everything about them from story-telling aspects to massive worlds open to exploration. After delving into almost every genre, I find myself favoring Role-playing games, 3D Platformers, and Action-Adventure. I have been gaming ever since I was given a Playstation at the age of 5. I have many fond memories glued to the TV screen until early hours of the morning. The alluring mysteries of game worlds these stories took place in completely captivated me. Gaming also provides my with an outlet to socialize. I have met so many wonderful people through online platforms (such as Xbox Live). Through the medium of gaming, I ended up discovering my love of technology.
